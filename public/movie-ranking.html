The Best Movies According to Harini

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Five Feet Apart

Two teenagers with cystic fibrosis fall in love but must keep a safe distance due to their illness. Their emotional journey explores love,loss and the value of human connection despite physical boundaries.

La La Land

A jazz musician and an aspiring actress chase their dreams in Los Angeles while navigating love , ambition, and sacrifice. The film blends vibrant visuals and music to potray the bittersweet beauty of following passion.

Fault In Our Stars

Hazel and Gus, two teens with cancer, embark on a powerful love story after meeting at a support group. Their relarionship is tender,heart wrenching, and filled with moments that challenge the meaning of life and loss.
